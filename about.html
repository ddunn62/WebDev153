About

01 Mission

Our mission

nightlens is a shared astronomy space built for curious beginners and dedicated sky-watchers. The goal is simple: make astronomy easier to explore through curated reading, community image sharing, and practical gear guidance.

02 Features

What you can do here

Browse the Feed for curated astronomy articles, join the Forum to post and discuss astrophotography, and use Resources to find telescope, eyepiece, and camera references.

Curated feed Image forum Gear resources

03 Community

Community guidelines

Be respectful, give helpful feedback, and share information clearly. When posting images, include basic capture details so others can learn from your setup.
